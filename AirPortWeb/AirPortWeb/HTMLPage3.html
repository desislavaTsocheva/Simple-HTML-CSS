Plovdiv was recently named the oldest city in Europe, with a history dating back more than 6000 years.
The city and its surroundings abound of cultural, archaeological, historical and natural attractions.
All year around the city is the host of musical, art and cultural festivals, concerts and forums.
The Rhodopes provide conditions for mountain, ecological, hunting and fishing tourism.
